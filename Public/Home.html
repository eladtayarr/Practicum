Our Agents

About our real estate agents.

Gil Nissim

Gil Nissim has a lot of knowledge and experience in the business field. Gil has a professional background in the world of business in Israel and abroad, which includes an extensive network of personal connections with the Jewish community in Western Europe. He shares the extensive knowledge he has accumulated over the years in various lectures in the field of business and real estate. Also, he was born in Tel Aviv and grew up and was educated in the northern Tel Aviv area, therefore he also knows very well the needs of the residents of the area and the advantages that the area has to offer.

Oved Zangi

Oved Zangi was in the past a vice president at Zalman Shuval and Shlomo Gropman's Fair Fund and has about 30 years of experience in the real estate field. Among other things, he is the marketing of large and sought-after projects, including the deal for the most expensive penthouse in Israel worth about NIS 80 million, the Yarkon 96 project. Among his biggest clients in the world you can also find the famous Kardashian family. In addition, Oved is an expert in marketing to the Jewish communities abroad and has worked with many countries in the world, including France, Moscow, Germany, the United States, South Africa and other countries in the world.
